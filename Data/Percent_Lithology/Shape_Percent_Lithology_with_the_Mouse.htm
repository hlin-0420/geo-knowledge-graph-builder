Shape Percent Lithology with the Mouse

Once the percent lithology data has been loaded it can be shaped with the mouse. 

  1. On the Lithology tab, select Shape %Litho with Mouse.
    The cursor arrow will become a pencil.

  2. On the percent lithology track, click and drag on a boundary to shape.

    1. If a lithology has been reduced to zero percent, click and drag on the left of the boundary to pull the left-hand lithology to the left, and click and drag on  the right of the boundary to pull the right-hand lithology to the right). If the lithology on the far right is reduced to zero percent, hold CTRL and click to the left of the boundary - the left lithology will jump to this point.

    2. To prevent a curve from being shaped above or below a certain point, right click on the point (in the percent lithology track) and select Lock Shaping, then Above or Below to lock the boundaries in the desired direction.

    3. To shape a specific depth range, right click on a point (in the percent lithology track) and select Lock Shaping, then Enter Depths - define a Start and End Depth and click OK.

    4. To revert back to a fully shapeable percent lithology, right click in the percent lithology track and select Unlock Shaping.

  3. To exit this mode, hit Esc on the keyboard.
