Export Percent Lithology to ASCII

  1. Double click on the % Lithology track, or on the Lithology tab select the % Lithology track. The Percentage Lithology Information dialog box will open.Â 

  2. Select specific rows you wish to export by clicking on the grey, numbered cell to the right of the row. To export all rows, select the cell in the top left corner.

  3. To include a start and end depth for each row of data, tick Index Range in each row. Clear to just include the start depth.

  4. Click Export.
    Note. Any percent lithology descriptions will also be exported.

  5. Open notepad or a similar application and paste the exported data.
