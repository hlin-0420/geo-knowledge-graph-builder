Gas Quality Ratio

Name

Gas Quality Ratio

 

Description

Compares the Total Gas value against the Sum of the Chromatograph data. Used to help calibrate the two sensors and shows unreliable data. (Results between 0.8 and 1.2 are reliable)

 

Formula

GQR = TGPPM/(C1+(2*C2)+(3*C3)+(4*(iC4+nC4))+(5*(iC5+nC5)))
