Move Modifiers

Select the modifier and drag it to its new position.

Or, 

On the Home tab, select Arrow Keys Nudge. Use the arrow keys to move the modifier to its new position.

 

Move modifier with precision

  1. Right click on the modifier and select Modifier Properties.
    The Accessory Information dialog box will open.

  2. Enter the depth in the At box.

  3. Click Update and Exit.
