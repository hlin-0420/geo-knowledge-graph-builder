Contact us

Call or E-mail

We have support centers strategically located around the world, so please feel free to contact whichever one of them is most suitable to your location and time zone. 

 

A list of phone numbers can be found here.

 

Alternatively, you can e-mail support@geologix.com  or Submit a Ticket.

 

On making contact, please provide the support staff with your license number and current software version. 

 

Customer Portal
Login to the customer portal to find all the help and assistance you need about our software products. You can also reference release notes and monitor your support tickets.

  1. Go to https://www.geologix.com/contact-us/.

  2. Scroll down to the Customer Portal section and follow the steps to create an account.

  3. Once your account is created, simply browse to https://support.geologix.com/support/login and click Login Here - enter your email address and password and click Sign in.

 

Website

Geologix.com provides up-to-date information on our products and services, as well as downloadable updates, utilities, FAQs, and support for authorized licence holders of our products.

 

GEO Training Courses

Did you know that Geologix provides free basic training or advanced-feature tuition at our offices in Norwich, Houston, Jakarta, Mumbai or Rio?

This is the best way to learn how to use the GEO Software and is available to all licensed-users and their nominated consultants.

Check out the support section of our website, or contact your local Geologix office for further details on training & tuition.
