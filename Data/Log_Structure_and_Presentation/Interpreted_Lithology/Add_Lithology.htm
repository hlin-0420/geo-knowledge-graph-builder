Add Lithology

  1. On the Home tab, select the lithology from the Interpreted Lithology palette.
    Use the arrows to view more lithology types.

  2. Place the cursor over the lithology track at the lithology start depth. 

  3. Left click and hold, drag to the lithology end depth and then release.
    Lithology can be inserted lithology onto an empty section of track or as interbeds within an existing lithology unit. 
    The cursor will become a when inserting lithology onto an empty section of track and a when inserting interbeds.
