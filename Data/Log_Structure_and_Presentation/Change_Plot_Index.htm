Change Plot Index

The plot index can be depth or date-time based.

Note. Once an ODF has been saved in date-time or depth mode, you cannot convert to the other.

  1. Open a new session of GEO. The plot type will be detailed in the bottom status bar.

  2. On the GEO menu, select either Date Time -> Depth or Depth -> Date Time as required.Â 

Note. Some advanced features in GEO may be disabled in date-time mode, such as edit/copy special tracks.
